# E-Commerce Customer & Sales Analysis — Turkey

## About
Analysis of e-commerce transaction data from Turkey covering customer 
retention, buying behavior, delivery performance, and revenue trends 
across 8 product categories and 10 cities.

## Questions Answered
1. Category wise customer retention rate
2. Gender wise retention rate
3. Age group wise retention rate
4. Most bought category by gender
5. Most bought category by age group
6. Average delivery time per category
7. Delivery time impact on customer rating
8. Month by month revenue trend
9. Best performing category each month
10. Year over year revenue comparison
11. Spending and payment method differences (returning vs new customers)
12. Session behavior differences (returning vs new customers)
13. Discount impact on customer retention

## Key Findings
- Retention is broadly consistent across categories, genders, and age groups
- Electronics dominates revenue every single month
- Delivery time has no measurable impact on customer rating
- Discounts do not appear to drive customer retention

## Tools Used
- MySQL: data storage and querying
- Python: pandas, SQLAlchemy
- Jupyter Notebook: analysis and presentation
- Power BI: dashboard
